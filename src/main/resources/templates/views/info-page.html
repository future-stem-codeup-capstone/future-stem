Info page

What?

Future STEM is an interactive and engaging platform targeted for your Future STEM student. Your Stem student can find engaging project ideas to do at home then choose to display his/her interpretation on his/her profile so others can check them out!

How?

  • Username - This username is only displayed on your own profile and when a project is posted
  • Email - This email address Should be of the parent or guardian of the Stem student.
  • Password - Parent or guardian should make the password and write it down and keep it in a safe

Why?

FutureStem believes in connections. Our primary mission was to bring the parent and student closer together, in a way that has the parents being an active participant in the students' education. By asking more meaningful questions about the students interests inside and out of the classroom.
